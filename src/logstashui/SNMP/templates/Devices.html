Setup Instructions

To get started with SNMP device monitoring, follow these steps:

Step 1: Add a Device

Click the "Add Device" button and fill out the form with your device information. You will be prompted to setup a credential, network, or connection if you haven't set those up yet.

Step 2: Get your pipeline name

When you've added your device, close this window and click on the text in the Network column to copy your pipeline name. Every network has its own unique pipeline name.

Step 3: Add it to your logstash.yml file

In your Logstash.yml file, add the pipeline name you copied in step 2 to the xpack.management.pipeline.id list in your configuration file, then restart Logstash.

Tip

Every network has a unique pipeline name. You can add one or many to a single instance of Logstash. You must have this pipeline added to your config for the devices to be monitored.
